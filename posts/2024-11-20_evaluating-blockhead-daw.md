# Evaluating Blockhead DAW

I have been following and supporting this project on Patreon for a little while now.

![](images/2024-11-20_evaluating-blockhead-daw_blockhead.png)

I just downloaded v0.42.2 and I’m playing around with it. I think it’s been half a year since I looked at this last.

[https://www.patreon.com/c/colugomusic/posts](https://www.patreon.com/c/colugomusic/posts)

I followed the development on Youtube here [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p_Vj3wRS0sM&list=PLH9iPQ3SdYDKcFBd52FGC3rbSwRuPyHGn](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p_Vj3wRS0sM&list=PLH9iPQ3SdYDKcFBd52FGC3rbSwRuPyHGn)

I think this is the overview [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P5fWPBOdrY8](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P5fWPBOdrY8)

Something about this app intrigued me in the beginning, but I’m kind of lost now figuring it out again. I will have to watch some more of his streams and reconstruct my mental model.

In any case it’s interesting to see some leftfield audio dev happening again. There was golden age back when Jeskola Buzz was happening and so many tracker developers working on things.

I think there is this notion of macros where a whole set of clips and effects can be put inside of a macro. Macros can contain other macros. I think this flexibility could be really cool but I haven’t wrapped my brain around how it would be useful yet.
