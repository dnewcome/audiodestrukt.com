# Edirol PCR-30 keyboard repair

I’ve had to tear down my PCR-30 more times than I can count now. I’m starting to wonder why I keep putting up with this keyboard. If I didn’t like the feel of it so much I’d probably ditch it in favor of something else. In any case here are some photos of one of my recent repair jobs involving painting the contacts with automotive window defroster repair paint.
