# Korg &#8211; the old new thing

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.korg.com/uploads/Images/02MDelayStraight-on_634558649127154850.png)

I’m digging some of the new stuff that Korg is putting out. They have always been one of the most innovative gear companies in my opinion. I’m not a shill for them, I’m just looking out for cool stuff.

Some of the boutique synth makers like Dave Smith instruments might have some flashier stuff, but from a product design standpoint Korg is killing it. I just worry that they aren’t popular enough for them to continue making this stuff.

The two things that are impressing me at the moment are the new [Mini Kaoss Pad 2](http://korg.com/minikaosspad2) and the [Monotron](http://www.korg.com/monotron) analog synths.

I think that we are going to be fighting over some of these little boxes in 20 years the way we are clamoring for TB303s these days. I just have a feeling.
