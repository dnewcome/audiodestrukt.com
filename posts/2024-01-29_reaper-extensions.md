# Reaper extensions

I’m a huge fan of Reaper for recording audio. I’ve been using it now for years and there’s always more I’m learning about it. It’s probably the most flexible audio app in terms of patching and routing, but I’ve always found the routing system a little on the complex side mostly owing to the way the little pop-out dialogs work. Once you get the hang of it it’s easy but initially it seems a little fiddly.

Recently I have wanted to check out some community extensions so I installed https://www.sws-extension.org/ This is required to get the extensions menu in the reaper GUI. I also grabbed reapack and put it in UserExtensions https://reapack.com/

Using ReaPack I installed MK Slicer and then added its action to the extensions menu using the reaper actions dialog.

Following this guy’s video https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oa-81nimeb8
