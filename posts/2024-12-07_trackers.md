# Trackers

I’m a fan of trackers. Mostly out of nostalgia now. I never did get much music written back in the day using trackers but I still liked dabbling. FastTracker 2 was a revelation seeing those oscilloscope displays dancing away.

There was something I found called GoatStudio that was a simple curses-based tracker. I have been digging into a few programming projects recently and realized that I ran across another tracker of that type called PLEB Tracker.
